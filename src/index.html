    Reggaeducaton Concert Tour 2023

    Get ready to vibe and make a real impact with the hottest reggaeton tour in town, putting your dance moves to work for our local schools. Let's hit the floor, and turn up the education! Groove to the infectious tunes while knowing that your ticket purchase is helping to pave the way for a brighter future. Proceeds from this unforgettable concert tour will directly benefit our community's education initiatives, providing vital resources, scholarships, and opportunities for students of all ages. Every move you make on the dance floor contributes to building a stronger foundation for learning and growth.

    26.08(SAT) ~ 30.12(SAT)

    @ Arsenal Studio, Ivan de Bodout Colisseum, and more.
    Are you vendor that would like to partner with our events? Contact us via email: reggaeducaton2023@mail.com
